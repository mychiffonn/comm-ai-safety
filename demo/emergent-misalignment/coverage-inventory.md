# Coverage inventory

> Search cutoff: July 18, 2026. This is all coverage located under the protocol in [`.provenance.md`](.provenance.md), not a claim of global exhaustiveness.

## Coding notes

- **Independent:** outlet selected and edited its own report or feature.
- **Commissioned/commentary:** expert analysis or opinion, not straight reporting.
- **Interested:** author, publisher, university, research group, or other institution connected to the work.
- **Derivative:** wire copy, translation, aggregation, adaptation, or AI-assisted summary. Source-family members are not counted as separate independent corroboration.
- **Full / partial / inventory:** full text was inspectable; material excerpts or a reliable derivative were inspectable; or only discovery metadata was available. `Inventory` items remain unverified in the audit.
